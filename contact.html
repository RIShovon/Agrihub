Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our emergency line.

Is there a cost for consulting with your experts?

Basic consultations are free. For extensive on-site visits or detailed analysis, there may be a nominal fee which will be discussed upfront.

Can you help with specific crop diseases?

Yes, our plant pathologists can help identify diseases and recommend treatment plans. Please include clear photos with your inquiry.

Do you provide soil testing services?

We offer soil testing and analysis. You can either visit our lab or mail us soil samples following our collection guidelines.

Are your services available to farmers outside the local area?

Yes, we provide remote consultations via video call, email, and phone for farmers anywhere in the country.
