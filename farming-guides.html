Organic Farming Guide

Farming Methods

Learn sustainable farming practices without synthetic chemicals, focusing on soil health and ecological balance.

Step-by-Step Process:
Step 1: Soil Preparation

Test your soil and amend it with organic matter like compost and manure. Ensure proper pH balance for optimal plant growth.

Step 2: Crop Selection

Choose crops suited to your climate and soil conditions. Consider companion planting to naturally deter pests.

Step 3: Natural Pest Control

Implement integrated pest management using beneficial insects, crop rotation, and organic pesticides.

Step 4: Weed Management

Use mulching, hand weeding, and cover crops to control weeds without chemicals.

Best Practices:
  • Rotate crops annually to prevent soil depletion
  • Use compost tea to boost plant immunity
  • Maintain biodiversity with hedgerows and cover crops
  • Practice water conservation techniques

Seasonal Planting Guide

Seasons

Optimize your planting schedule for maximum yield throughout the year with this seasonal guide.

Spring Planting (March-May)
  • Tomatoes, peppers, eggplants
  • Leafy greens (lettuce, spinach)
  • Root vegetables (carrots, beets)
  • Herbs (basil, cilantro, parsley)
Summer Planting (June-August)
  • Beans, corn, cucumbers
  • Squash, melons, pumpkins
  • Okra, sweet potatoes
  • Heat-tolerant greens
Fall Planting (September-November)
  • Broccoli, cauliflower, cabbage
  • Garlic, onions, shallots
  • Leafy greens (kale, collards)
  • Root crops (turnips, radishes)
Winter Planning (December-February)
  • Plan next season's crops
  • Maintain soil with cover crops
  • Prune fruit trees and bushes
  • Prepare seed starting supplies
